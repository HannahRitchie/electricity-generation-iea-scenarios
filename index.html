This data reflects electricity generation per person across selected regions and countries today, and in the scenarios published by the International Energy Agency (IEA) for 2050.

Per capita values were calculated by dividing total electricity generation by population figures published by the IEA. This generation data includes electricity used for residential purposes, in industry, commercial and other services.

Based on data from International Energy Agency (2025), as modified by Hannah Ritchie. Population from IEA Global Energy and Climate Model inputs.
